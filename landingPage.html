Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

How do I find the right service provider?

Simply enter your location and the service you need. SevaLink will show you a list of verified professionals along with ratings, reviews, and availability. Choose the one that best fits your needs and book instantly.

Are the service providers reliable and skilled?

Yes! We thoroughly verify each professional through background checks, experience validation, and customer ratings. You can also check user reviews before booking.

What if I need urgent service?

SevaLink offers instant bookings for urgent needs. Just select the "Emergency Service" option, and we'll connect you with the nearest available professional.

Can I communicate with my service provider before they arrive?

Absolutely! Our in-app chat and call feature allows you to discuss details or special requests directly with the provider.

What if I have an issue with the service?

If you're not satisfied, you can report the issue within the app, and our support team will step in to help resolve it quickly.

Do I need to tip the service provider?

Tipping is optional but appreciated. If you're happy with the service, you can tip via the app or in cash.

What if my service provider cancels last minute?

If a provider cancels, we'll notify you immediately and help you find an alternative professional so your work doesn't get delayed.

How do I become a service provider on SevaLink?

Simple! Click "Join as a Provider", complete the verification process, and start getting job requests in your area.
